What’s a Men’s Shed?

Men’s Sheds are community-based, non-profit groups that provide a safe, friendly place to connect, build, repair, learn, and support each other. Everyone’s welcome — all skills and experience levels.

Typical activities

  • Woodworking & metalwork
  • Tool maintenance & community repairs
  • Mentoring & skills sharing
  • Catching up over tea/coffee
